ADMC

The AMC Learning Program (ALP) and De Mathematics Committee (DMC) present you the:

ALP x DMC Mathematics Competition

The ADMC (ALP x DMC Mathematics Competition) is an original competition brought to you by ALP (AMC Learning Program) and DMC (De Mathematics Competitions). Our mission is to provide a competition that contains both problems approachable for people who are just starting competition math and challenging for seasoned math competitors. The contest will be run on the Mathematical Advancement Committee's platform, admc.mathadvance.org; competitors will use this to access the competition rounds.

Format/Rules

There will be 4 rounds:

  • Individual Round #1: It is 10 problems in 45 minutes and can only be done by each competitor.
  • Individual Round #2: It is 10 problems in 60 minutes and, again, can only be done by each competitor. Also, it is harder than Individual Round #1.
  • Team Round: It is 10 problems in 60 minutes but can be done by four individual competitors in collaboration as a team.
  • Tiebreaker Round: TBD.
