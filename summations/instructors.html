Evan Chang

Head Instructor

Evan is a graduating freshman at the High Technology High School in Lincroft, New Jersey. He is the co-founder and the head of Content Creation for the Online Monmouth Math Competition. He is also a problem writer for the OYMC (Online Youth Math Competition).

Over the past year, he has received the following scores: 73 on the USAMTS, 141 on the AMC 10, and 10 on the AIME. He did qualify for the USAJMO, getting a score of 23.

You may find him as “squareman” on the AoPS forums. Outside of mathematics, he likes composing music as he was one of 7 finalists in the prestigious MTNA National Composition Competition. As you can probably tell, he a fond writer of a lot of summations.
