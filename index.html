The AMC Learning Program


ALP is a FREE math program offering detailed materials and resources for

students at the AMC level who want to get further in the math competition series.






Mission Statement

At ALP, we strive to provide high-quality materials and resources for interested and motivated students to learn from and grow. We wish to expose learners to an intuitive approach to concepts. We encourage learners to explore more and gain an in-depth understanding. We also want to provide resources that are easily accessible to communities across the world and build a whole community ourselves made up of innovative minds.
