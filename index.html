AMC Learning Program


A mathematical program offering detailed materials and lectures for students

learning at an AMC/AIME level who want to get further in the math competition series.






Mission Statement

At ALP, we strive to provide high-quality materials and lectures for interested and motivated students to learn from and grow. We wish to expose learners to an intuitive approach to concepts. We encourage learners to explore more and gain an in-depth understanding. We also want to provide resources that are easily accessible to communities across the world and build a whole community ourselves made up of innovative minds.
