Classes

The focal point of ALP is the live lectures. These lectures are for people who have taken the AMCs before and want to do better next year or who missed out this year and want to prepare for next year.

Students should be enthusiastic about math and wish to attend the lectures on their initiative. We educate students on learning math and pique their interest in the subject. However, we do not guarantee results, and that is not the essential focus of this program.

Logistics

This program covers the four main subjects: Algebra, Combinatorics, Geometry, and Number Theory. There will be four lectures for all four of these subjects, except Number Theory, which has five instead. Everything we cover is essential to that subject to the AMC (and possibly AIME).

There are two sessions. Each session lasts for four weeks, and each session covers two of the four previously mentioned subjects. Session one consists of Algebra and NT lectures, and Session two consists of Geometry and Combinatorics.

In each session, there are two lectures per week. One lecture belongs to one of the subjects, and the second lecture belongs to the other. The lectures under one subject are all taught by the same instructor, and the timing of them under one subject is the same time and date every week.

To attend a lecture, you must RSVP for it using the respective form found under the Materials
